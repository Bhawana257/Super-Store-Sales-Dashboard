📊 Super Store Sales Dashboard – Project Description

The Super Store Sales Dashboard is a data visualization project developed to analyze and monitor overall sales performance, profit, and customer trends of a retail business. The dashboard provides an interactive and comprehensive overview of key business metrics using charts, maps, and summary indicators.

🔹 Project Objectives

To analyze total sales, profit, and number of orders

To monitor monthly sales trends across different years

To identify top-performing states and regions

To evaluate sales by category and sub-category

To analyze sales distribution by segment and payment mode

To support data-driven decision-making

🔹 Key Features of the Dashboard
1️⃣ KPI Summary Section

The dashboard displays important performance indicators:

Ship Days

Total Orders (22K)

Total Sales (1.6M)

Total Profit (175K)

These KPIs provide a quick overview of business performance.

2️⃣ Sales & Profit by State (Map Visualization)

A geographical map shows state-wise sales and profit distribution across the United States.

Helps identify high-performing states

Supports regional sales strategy planning

3️⃣ Monthly Sales Analysis

Two line charts display:

Monthly Sales by Year (2019 vs 2020)

Overall Monthly Sales Trend

This helps in:

Identifying seasonal patterns

Comparing yearly growth

Forecasting future performance

4️⃣ Sales by Segment

A donut chart shows sales distribution among:

Consumer (Highest contribution)

Corporate

Home Office

This helps understand which customer segment generates more revenue.

5️⃣ Sales by Payment Mode

Displays distribution of payment methods:

COD (Highest)

Online

Cards

This helps analyze customer payment preferences.

6️⃣ Sales by Region

Shows sales distribution across:

West

East

Central

South

This supports regional performance evaluation.

7️⃣ Sales by Category & Sub-Category

Bar charts represent:

Category: Office Supplies, Technology, Furniture

Sub-Category: Phones, Chairs, Binders

These charts help identify the most profitable product categories.

🔹 Tools & Technologies Used

Microsoft Power BI / Excel (Dashboard Design)

Data Modeling

DAX / Pivot Tables

Interactive Filters (Region selection: Central, East, South, West)

Data Cleaning and Transformation
